About Brand

We connect creators — from zero followers to 50k+ — with PR packages and brand opportunities. We act as the middleman to validate, match and coordinate PR packages so creators can receive, post and grow their presence.

What we do

We operate as a curator and coordinator: we validate creator applications, match eligible creators with brands and manage the communication and shipping of PR packages. Creators do not contact brands directly — we handle brand relationships and logistics on their behalf.

How it works — step by step

  1. Complete a short questionnaire. This helps us evaluate fit for brand briefs.
  2. Enter your basic details & submit. Email, handle(s), and shipping region are required for validation.
  3. Complete required micro-tasks (3–5 deals). These are simple tasks you complete to help fund operations; they’re required before we forward your validated request to brands.
  4. If matched, we send PR packages. When you receive a package you are expected to post about it to the platform(s) you selected within 10 hours as required by many programs.

Selection & timeline

  • Shortlist system: Applications are shortlisted. Higher-fit creators (audience, content, engagement) are prioritized.
  • Review time: Expect an acceptance email within 72 hours.
  • Reapply: If not accepted, you can reapply after the 72-hour window.

Application limits

You may apply to up to three brands at a time. Each application is evaluated individually.

Supported platforms

We support content posted to TikTok and Instagram (feed, reels, stories). At this time we do not support Threads, Facebook or full-length YouTube (we do support short-form video where specified).

Eligibility & shipping

  • Regions: Currently creators must be located in United States, United Kingdom, Canada, or Australia for shipping and legal reasons.
  • Age: You must be 18+ to participate in most brand programs.
  • Account: Public or business creator accounts are preferred for validation.

What we ask of creators

  • Complete the questionnaire honestly so we can match you accurately.
  • Finish the required 3–5 simple tasks before your request is forwarded to brands.
  • Post about received PR packages within 10 hours on the agreed platform(s).

Clarifications

Are opportunities guaranteed?

No — placements use a shortlist and matching process based on fit. Being eligible does not guarantee selection.

Do I contact brands directly?

No — we act as the intermediary. We coordinate shipping and communication between qualified creators and brands.

Can I update my handle or region?

Yes — update your profile promptly. Region changes may affect eligibility due to shipping constraints.
